Halloween Today

Today, Halloween is celebrated across many parts of the world, though it's most prominent in North America. It's a night of fun and fright with trick-or-treating, costume parties, haunted houses, and pumpkin carving. Modern celebrations are rooted in centuries of folklore, but now emphasize community and creativity.

People of all ages dress up as their favorite characters, both scary and fun. It's a time for spooky stories, indulging in candy, and celebrating the unknown.

The Origins of Costumes

Costumes have been a part of Halloween for centuries, dating back to the belief that wearing disguises could help hide individuals from spirits. Today, costumes are worn not to ward off spirits, but for fun. Popular costumes have evolved over the years, from witches and ghosts to superheroes and fictional characters.
